Welcome to Tea Stall - a cozy and delightful tea haven dedicated to providing you with a unique tea experience. Nestled in the heart of Rupushdi, our small tea stall is a hidden gem where you can escape the hustle and bustle of daily life and indulge in the art of tea appreciation.

At Tea Stall, we believe that tea is not just a beverage but a journey of flavors and aromas. We carefully curate a diverse selection of teas sourced from renowned tea gardens around the world. From classic black teas to soothing herbal blends and refreshing green teas, our menu offers something to please every tea connoisseur.

We take pride in our commitment to quality, ensuring that every cup of tea is crafted with precision and care. Our skilled tea masters use traditional brewing techniques to extract the full essence of each tea, ensuring a rich and satisfying experience with every sip.

Whether you're a tea enthusiast seeking to expand your palate or a casual tea lover looking for a comforting drink, our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always ready to guide you through our tea selection, recommending the perfect brew to suit your taste preferences.

Step into our charming tea stall, and allow the tranquil ambiance to envelop you as you embark on a sensory journey through the world of tea. Join us and discover the magic that lies within each cup at Tea Stall.
